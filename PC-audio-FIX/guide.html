Settings For Great Audio


Turn off sound enhancements


Turn this setting off If you are running external audio devices.


Turn off all software control


To make sure no background software Is running, unckeck this setting. For Spacial audio, keep It on.


  1. Go to control panel
  2. Change to small icons
  3. Click on Sound
  4. Click on the speaker you are using
  5. You will find the setting in the advance menu as image Is shown
  6. After unchecked, click apply.
